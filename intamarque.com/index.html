e-commerce store

The online store

Whatever time zone you’re in, Intamarque’s wholesale webshop is open. We keep over 500 brands constantly stocked and refreshed so we can guarantee everything is current. Even if you can’t see the product you’re after, call us. We’ll do our best to get it for you, whether it’s listed on our site or not.

Our online store, shows current, front and back product images of all stock – front and back – so you can check the labelling, product information, country of origin and instructions for use. Keeping these details up to date and accurate is a priority for us. What you see online is what you get. Creating an account is easy and you will be verified in 24 hours.
