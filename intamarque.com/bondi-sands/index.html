Bondi Sands – Self Tan, Suncare & Skincare

Our buying and marketing personnel work closely with manufacturers of all sizes not just to sell products but also to build brands. For example, as well as handling well-known products such as Nivea, Dove, Johnson’s, Oral-B, Pampers and Listerine, we have also been actively involved in the development of new brands such as Argan Oil beauty products and Bondi Sands Self Tan.

For brands in their infancy, or wanting to enter the UK market, Intamarque can offer either a distribution service, or full brand building offering. Our vast customer base ensures that brands are well represented across all channels, be that wholesale, pharmacy, high street retailers, online retailers or grocery multiples.

 
 
 

BONDI SANDS TESTIMONIAL

“Our partnership with Intamarque commenced in 2014 with a joint focus to become the leading self tanning brand within the United Kingdom. With Intamarque’s brand and commercial expertise Bondi Sands is now the number 1 self tanning brand in the UK. Our rapid rise  to success are ingredients of Intamarque’s  passionate hard working team, strong retail relationships and the ability to align their business practices to our brands objectives and overarching vision.”

SHAUN WILSON BONDI SANDS
