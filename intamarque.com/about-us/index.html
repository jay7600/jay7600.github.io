About Intamarque

Intamarque are innovation-driven and consumer-led. As a leading UK
wholesale distributor of branded products, we understand your market and
your consumer. But our reach is global.

Customer-led. Solution-driven

Our wholesale distribution services provide you with full support at every level, so you get a first class customer experience. We champion innovative, tech-driven solutions to supply and distribute goods in over 60 countries. We use our heads-up market intelligence to spot the trends and brands of tomorrow.

Over 15 years, we’ve built a business that is lean, streamlined and customer - obsessed. Our innovative sales support team means we will go the extra mile to deliver, wherever you need us to, whatever the challenges.
