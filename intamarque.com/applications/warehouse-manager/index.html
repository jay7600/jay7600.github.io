Warehouse Operative

The Role

 

  • Replenishment of pallets
  • Picking orders
  • Putting pallets away in to the racking
  • Stacking container orders
  • Cleaning the Warehouse

  

Essential experience

 

  • Experience of working within a fast paced Warehouse environment
  • Excellent communication skills
  • A keen willingness to learn

  

Essential Abilities:

 

  • Enthusiastic
  • Self-motivated
  • Able to work under pressure
  • Time management
  • Flexible Approach
  • The ability to work well within a team environment as well as individually

  

Job Summary

 

Job Title: Warehouse Operative
Division: Warehouse
Location: Head Office, Tewkesbury
Reporting to: Warehouse Manager
 

Hours: 40 Hours Per Week Monday to Friday

Salary: Negotiable, depending upon experience

 

The Person

Due to the continuing growth and success of Intamarque, we now have a fantastic opportunity for a Warehouse Operative to join our Warehouse team in Tewkesbury. You will have experience of working within a busy warehouse environment and will be
involved in a variety of activities to assist and ensure the efficient processing of Goods In and Out of the Warehouse.

FLT Licences Counterbalance, Bendi or PPT would be an advantage, however, not essential as full training or refresher training can be provided. to assist the Warehouse Manager, to ensure all customer and business requirements are met
